About Us

We are more than a tour company — we are curators of unforgettable journeys across Kenya's most iconic landscapes.

Our Story

MasMara Tour Agency was founded with a passion for Africa’s wild beauty and a deep respect for its people, wildlife, and landscapes. Born in the heart of the Maasai Mara, we aim to connect travelers with authentic and transformative safari experiences.

Our Mission

To deliver immersive, ethical, and personalized safaris that celebrate Kenya’s rich biodiversity while empowering local communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading safari agency known for integrity, sustainability, and exceptional customer experiences in East Africa.

Our Core Values

  • Authenticity – We believe in real, raw, and meaningful travel.
  • Sustainability – Every safari supports conservation and local livelihoods.
  • Excellence – From booking to bush, we provide seamless service and local expertise.
  • Respect – For nature, culture, and every traveler’s unique journey.

Meet the Team

Our team of expert guides, travel consultants, and local partners work together to craft unforgettable experiences. Many of our guides are native to the regions they explore, bringing unmatched insight and passion to every tour.
