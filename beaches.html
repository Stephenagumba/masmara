Top Beaches to Visit in Kenya

Explore the stunning coastal gems of Kenya, handpicked by MasMara Travel Agency for your perfect tropical escape.

Diani Beach

Famous for its white sands, coral reefs, and palm-lined coast, Diani is a must-visit destination.

Watamu Beach

Known for its marine national park and vibrant coral gardens, great for snorkeling and diving.

Malindi Beach

A blend of rich history and pristine beach with a vibrant Swahili culture and great resorts.

Nyali Beach

Close to Mombasa, Nyali offers serene beaches, surf spots, and stylish resorts.

Lamu Beach

A peaceful island with timeless charm, perfect for a relaxing escape and cultural exploration.

Tiwi Beach

Less crowded and serene, Tiwi is ideal for couples and nature lovers seeking privacy.
